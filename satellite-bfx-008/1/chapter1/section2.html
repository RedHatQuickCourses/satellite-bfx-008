- displaying dynflow-sidekiq@worker-1              [FAIL]
- displaying dynflow-sidekiq@worker-hosts-queue-1  [FAIL]

Here is the hammer ping output:

Error: Timed out reading data from server

Objectives

  • Investigate why the services are in failed state.

  • Solve the issue related to the services.

Recreate Scenario

To recreate this scenario, run the lab script on the bastion server.

lab break breakfix2
